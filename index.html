about us

History

Asea Spa is an international spa operator company created in 2005. Aurelie Denoueix, her Fondator, changed her life after living in Bali for few years. She found in love with the magic Island, the Balinese people and their ancestral therapies and natural recipes. She decided to share her experience with others and provide the benefits of The Asian Tropical Spa.

Managing your Spa

When you partner with Asea Spa, you gain more than just an association with any spa operator. The ultimate aim is to develop a team of professional, loyal and dedicated employees who will strive to enhance the guests' experience by delivering nothing less than the 'perfect touch'. Naturally, while we are adding all of those extra touches to your guest's experience, we will also be ensuring that you are making a profit. To explore how a partnership with Asea Spa can benefit you, contact us.
